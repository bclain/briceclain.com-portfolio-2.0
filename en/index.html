Market analysis

Competitors mainly target a female audience. Additionally, their design is mostly very generic/concrete: few illustrations, rather "cold", especially when ecology is emphasized.

Problem

The target audience is broad. Therefore, we must speak to all genders without losing the audience defined as "female".

Solution

Highlight the product

Highlighting its usefulness allows addressing a neutral clientele.
